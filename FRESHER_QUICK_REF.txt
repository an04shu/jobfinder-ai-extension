💡 SETUP TIPS FOR FRESHERS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

JOB ROLE:
   ✅ "Software Engineer" (not "Senior Software Engineer")
   ✅ "Data Analyst" (not "Lead Data Analyst")
   ✅ "Frontend Developer" (not "Principal Developer")

SKILLS:
   ✅ List 3-7 relevant skills
   ✅ Include technologies from projects/courses
   ✅ Example: "Python, React, SQL, Git, Docker"

EXPERIENCE:
   ✅ 0 if fresh graduate
   ✅ 1 if one internship
   ⚠️  Be honest - triggers right search level

GRADUATION:
   ✅ 2024, 2025, or 2026 for recent grads
   ✅ Triggers campus hiring keywords

LOCATION:
   ✅ "Remote" for maximum opportunities
   ✅ Or leave blank for all locations
