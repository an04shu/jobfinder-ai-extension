💡 SMART OPTIONAL FIELD HANDLING:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

How Optional Fields Work:

1. SKILLS FIELD
   Filled: "Python, React"
     → Query: "(Python OR React)"
     → Searches jobs mentioning these skills
   
   Blank: 
     → Query: (no skills filter)
     → Searches ALL skills

2. EXPERIENCE FIELD
   Filled: 0
     → Query: "entry level OR junior OR intern"
     → Targets entry-level jobs
   
   Filled: 5
     → Query: "mid-level OR intermediate"
     → Targets mid-level jobs
   
   Blank:
     → Query: (no experience filter)
     → Searches ALL levels

3. LOCATION FIELD
   Filled: "Remote"
     → Query: "Remote"
     → Only remote jobs
   
   Blank:
     → Query: (no location filter)
     → ALL locations
